# WorkLog Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026

WorkLog is designed to store your work location logs on your devices and, when iCloud is enabled, sync them through your personal iCloud account.

## Data WorkLog Handles

WorkLog may store the following information you create in the app:

- Work log dates
- Location names, suburbs, or coordinates saved for a log
- Paid or unpaid status
- Notes you add manually
- App preferences, such as filters and dismissed prompts

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, WorkLog uses Apple's CloudKit service to sync your logs between devices signed in to your Apple ID. Your WorkLog data is stored in your private iCloud database. The developer does not operate a separate server for this data.

## Location

WorkLog asks for location permission only when you use features that save your current location. Location data is used to create work logs and show saved logs on the map. WorkLog does not use location data for advertising or tracking.

## Tracking and Advertising

WorkLog does not track you across apps or websites. WorkLog does not include third-party advertising.

## Data Deletion

You can delete logs inside the app. You can also remove WorkLog's iCloud data from your Apple ID iCloud storage settings.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support details listed on WorkLog's App Store page.
